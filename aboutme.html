About me

Maíra Sá Araújo

My name is Maíra Sá Araújo. I was born in 20/10/1992 in Uberlândia, Minas Gerais - Brazil. I'm 29 years old now and I have a degree in Law back home. As I've never felt really happy in my home country, I decided to leave everything behind and start a new life in Europe. I decided to move to Ireland in November/December of 2019 and I moved to Cork on March 2020. Right after I arrived, the first lock-down started.

I started an online programming course and decided that I could learn it in order to find a good job and settle in Ireland for life, and is also a knowledge that I've always had interest in having.

Now I've just finished my second year in Computer Science and I'm looking for a job in the area to have more real life experience.
